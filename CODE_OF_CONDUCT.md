# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

navigaT-SQL adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct. The full text is published at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

In short: we pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and respectful
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We expect contributors to act
with empathy and good faith, accept constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is
best for the community.

## Reporting

Concerns about conduct in this project's spaces may be reported privately to the maintainers
via [@maui314159](https://github.com/maui314159). Reports will be reviewed and handled
confidentially and fairly, following the Contributor Covenant's enforcement guidelines.
